2024 Recruitment for Prospective PhD Students

Our group sits at the intersection of multiple disciplines in research, which includes the integration of multiscale modeling, machine learning, and simulations of various systems. We actively collaborate with both experimental and computational experts. Currently, we are seeking 2-3 motivated students to join our lab starting Fall 2024. This opportunity is open for PhD students in the Chemistry, Chemical Engineering, or Materials Science programs. No prior computational experience is needed to join the lab! More details are descibed in this PPTX.

 

We encourage you to schedule a 1-on-1 prospective meeting with Dr. Sarupria to learn more about the opportunities described above. You can schedule a 30-minute meeting with her via this link. If there are no slots available, you can contact Dr. Sarupria via her email.

 

You are also welcome to reach out to current students in the lab to gain perspective on what a day-to-day research looks like.
