Statistical Thermodynamics & Molecular Simulations (STMS) Seminar Series

This seminar series, organized by Dr. Sapna Sarupria (Dept. of Chemistry, UMN) and Dr. Amir Haji-Akbari (Dept. of Chemical and Environmental Engineering, Yale U) is aimed at providing a virtual platform for sharing scientific research in the area of statistical mechanics, molecular simulations, and computational materials science. Follow the link below for more information about the seminar's schedule and speakers.

 

Link to STMS Seminar Schedule: https://sites.google.com/view/stms2021/schedule?authuser=0

STMS Seminar Series Youtube Channel

Previous STMS seminar recordings can be found on the STMS Seminar Series Youtube Channel: https://www.youtube.com/@stmsseminarseries49/videos

i-CoMSE Workshop Information

The i-CoMSE institute aims to provide basic and advanced training needs of computational molecular science and engineering community through in-person and virtual schools, and well-curated training materials.

 

For information regarding workshop news and application cycles, please navigate to: https://www.i-comse.org/

 

For access to training materials, please navigate to the i-CoMSE GitHub repository at: https://github.com/icomse
